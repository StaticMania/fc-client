    Support Options

    We’ve got lots of ways we can stay in touch and get all the wrinkles, questions, comments ironed out and answered.

    Documentation

    We offer a complete set of documenation geared to end-users and administrator. Please visit the Federated Documentation Project.

    Support System

    Do you need support with your installation? Maybe you want to ask a question before purchase? Click one of the following three options to route your question properly…

    2. I have a question or issue before I become a customer. (sales@federated.computer)

    3. I want to talk with someone at Federated about something else. (info@federated.computer)

    Don’t worry, you can’t make a wrong choice.
